How it works

One platform. Three journeys.

Uprise connects students, companies and universities via tests, projects, mentorship and a shared talent marketplace.

For Students & Young Professionals

01
Create your profile

Add skills, interests, hobbies, sectors, certificates and ID verification. Connect your LinkedIn and portfolio.

02
Take tests & challenges

Logic tests, skill assessments, personality tests and real-case mini projects earn you XP and badges.

03
Join mentorship & projects

Enter 3-month mentorships, monthly challenges and real projects to level up and showcase your growth.

For Companies & HR

01
Set your hiring needs

Define roles, sectors (IT, AI, marketing, HR, finance, engineering etc.), required skills and seniority.

02
Browse ranked profiles

Filter by XP, test scores, projects, mentor feedback and availability. Shortlist the best matches.

03
Invite & rate talent

Run your own challenges, interview candidates, and rate their performance to improve the ecosystem.

For Universities & Faculties

01
Connect cohorts

Link student accounts with your university and study programmes for easy tracking and reporting.

02
Assign tests & projects

Use Uprise tests and challenges as coursework or optional extra-credit assignments.

03
Track outcomes

Monitor skill development, project completion and hiring outcomes per student or cohort.

Mentorship program

3-month intensive mentorship, built for real careers.

Work with mentors from IT, AI, marketing, management, design, HR, finance, engineering and more — through weekly sessions and project-based learning.

How mentorship works

  • Pick a sector: IT, AI, marketing, design, HR, finance, engineering, etc.
  • Match with a mentor based on goals and skill level.
  • Weekly sessions (online or in-person, depending on location).
  • Clear roadmap, exercises and project deliverables.
  • Feedback, rating, and portfolio-ready outcomes.

How projects work

  • Students apply or get matched to relevant projects.
  • Mentors or company reps guide expectations and deliverables.
  • Projects are scoped for 1–8 weeks.
  • Upon completion, students earn XP, ratings and portfolio items.
  • Companies can hire top performers directly from the platform.
